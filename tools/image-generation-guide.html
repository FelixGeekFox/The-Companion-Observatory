Image Generation Guide

A practical framework for creating visually consistent companion images across multiple scenes, moods and outputs.

Prompt Architecture

Use the guide as a repeatable checklist. Define the visual canon first, then build the final prompt as one cohesive image brief.

  1. Define the core subject and one-sentence image goal.
  2. Lock character profiles and continuity details.
  3. Choose the exact art style, lighting and rendering finish.
  4. Specify composition, camera, environment and mood.
  5. Add negative prompts and output requirements.

1. Core Subject

State what is being depicted: character, environment, object, scene or abstract concept. Condense the goal into one clear sentence, such as “a quiet rooftop confession scene at dusk between two rivals-turned-lovers.”

2. Character Profile

For each character, define age range, body type, height relationship, face shape, skin tone, eye colour, hair, distinguishing features, posture, clothing, textures, accessories, expression and body language. Avoid vague words like “attractive” or “cool”; use visible specifics.

3. Art Style

Style determines much of the output. Specify medium, realism level, line quality, lighting style, colour grading, texture and general visual influences without copying a specific copyrighted artist.

4. Composition & Framing

Define camera angle, aspect ratio, focal point, depth of field and movement. Mention whether the image is a close-up, mid-shot, wide cinematic frame, phone wallpaper or another format.

5. Environment & Mood

Describe location, time of day, weather and ambient details. Then describe the emotional temperature visually: how tension, tenderness, melancholy or triumph appears in posture, light, distance and atmosphere.

6. Continuity & Constraints

List invariants that must remain consistent across images, such as eye colour, hair streaks, clothing palette or rendering style. Add negative prompts for anatomy, watermarks, distorted hands, text overlays or unwanted style drift.

Companion Image Prompt Template

Use the attached reference images strictly for character design, facial features, hairstyles, proportions, height difference, and overall appearance. Maintain full character continuity.

Scene:
[Describe where the image takes place and what is happening.]

Character Placement & Interaction:
[Describe who is where, body language, hand placement, eye contact, closeness, movement, or emotional tension.]

Character Details:
[Describe outfits, expressions, props, accessories, and recognisable traits.]

Environment:
[Describe background details, objects, weather, architecture, atmosphere, and lived-in elements.]

Lighting:
[Describe the main light source, colour temperature, shadows, highlights, reflections, glow, haze, or rim light.]

Composition:
[Describe camera angle, shot type, framing, crop, perspective, depth of field, and aspect ratio.]

Style:
Polished semi-realistic anime-inspired cinematic illustration with soft painterly rendering, smooth skin shading, expressive eyes, detailed flowing hair, refined gradients, crisp edges, clean surfaces, minimal visible line art, cinematic lighting, shallow depth of field, atmospheric depth, and high-end digital rendering.

Rendering Constraints:
No grain. No noise. No rough texture. No painterly brush noise. No canvas texture. Clean surfaces. Smooth shading. Polished rendering throughout.

Avoid:
Extra limbs, duplicated fingers, warped hands, distorted anatomy, merged characters, broken perspective, inconsistent proportions, messy hair artifacts, rough textures, heavy outlines, cel shading, or unclear poses.

Mood:
[Describe the emotional tone of the image.]
